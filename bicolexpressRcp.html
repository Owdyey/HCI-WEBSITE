Bicol Express

Bicol express is a spicy Filipino stew consisting of bite sized pieces of pork with chilies, coconut milk, shrimp paste (bagoong), onions, tomato and garlic. The dish is named after the train in the Bicol region of the Philippines, known for its spicy food.

How to cook?

Ingredients

  • 21 tbsp cooking oil, as needed
  • 1 pc onion chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (not sweet)
  • ½ kg pork, cut into thin strips
  • 1 (40 g) pack Knorr Ginataang Gulay Mix dissolved
    in 1-½ cups water
  • 6 pcs green chillies, sliced

Procedure

  • Step 1:

    In a medium sized pot, get it nice and hot on medium-high heat. When hot enough, pour in a little oil and throw in the onions, garlic, ginger and shrimp paste.

  • Step 2:

    Mash the shrimp paste and sauté the vegetables until you can smell the lovely aroma from the combined ingredients.

  • Step 3:

    It’s time to add the pork. Allow the pork to cook for just a minute together with the aromatics (onions, garlic and ginger) to remove the gamey taste of the meat.

  • Step 4:

    You may now pour the dissolved Knorr Complete Recipe Mix Ginataang Gulay and bring this to a boil. Adjust your heat to low and cook this to simmer before adding the chilies.
